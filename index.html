Boots & Fish

Objective:
  • Move the boat and diver to gather the Boots and Fish to get as many points as possible in 60 seconds.
Scoring:
  • Boots are worth 5 points each.
  • Fish are worth from 40 (slowest) to 140 (fastest) point each depending on speed.
  • If a Boot hits the sea floor, you loose all the points you have accumulated.
Controls:
  • Use ← → arrows to move boat.
  • Use ↑ ↓ arrows to move diver.
